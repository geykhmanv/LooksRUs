We are LooksRUs

Our company was founded in 2018 by entrepreneur Nicholas Aponte and was initially launched solely for menswear. In 2019 womenswear was introduced by Victoria Geykhman who became the chief designer and joined forces with Aponte after becoming good friends in college. Our mission is to set the standard for affordable and fashion forward clothing as well as to project quality and comfort in the workplace. All LooksRUs clothing is exclusively designed in New York City and made at the highest standards for an affordable price. Our timeless, durable collections are made to suit your everyday lifestyle and has everything men and woman need to look stylish on a budget.

We are Trusted

We strive to produce stylish clothing that meets all your needs whilst being durable and comfortable. We adhere to high ethical standards and visit all factories we use to ensure that they are safe and high quality places.

We are Professional

We strongly value our customers opinions and have a full time customer service team ready to help. In everything we do, we prioritize you.

We are Experts

With many years of experience in apparel and running successful retail stores, our team is highly qualified in providing you with everything you need. From blouses to trousers, we create thoughtful clothing for those who want the comfort without sacrificing the style.
